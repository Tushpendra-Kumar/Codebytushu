FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is CodeByTushu? add
CodeByTushu is a platform designed to help developers learn, build projects, and grow their careers through practical resources and a supportive community.
Is CodeByTushu completely free? add
Most of our core learning resources and community features are completely free to ensure education is accessible to everyone.
Who can join CodeByTushu? add
Anyone! Whether you are a complete beginner or an experienced developer looking to upskill, you are welcome here.
Can beginners also learn? add
Absolutely. Our content is structured to take you from the very basics to advanced real-world applications.
How often is content updated? add
We add new courses, blogs, and challenges on a weekly basis to keep up with industry trends.
How can I contribute? add
You can contribute by participating in the community forums, writing blogs, or helping others with their coding challenges.
Why was CodeByTushu created? add
To provide a structured, noise-free learning environment that focuses on practical skills rather than just theory.
Is there a community to join? add
Yes! Join our growing community of developers through our Discord, YouTube, and social media channels for discussions and peer support.
