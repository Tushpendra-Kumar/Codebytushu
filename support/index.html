Support

Need help? We're here to assist you with any questions, issues, or feedback.

Support Overview
Our dedicated team is ready to help you navigate your journey. We usually respond within 24–48 hours to all inquiries.

1. Help & Support

At CodeByTushu, we are committed to providing you with the best possible learning experience. Whether you're struggling with a LeetCode problem, facing issues in a course, or have questions about our services, our support team is ready to assist you.

Please browse through our specific support channels below to find the most appropriate way to resolve your issue efficiently.

2. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Before reaching out to our support team, we recommend checking if your question has already been answered.

  • How do I access my purchased courses? You can access all your courses from the dashboard after logging into your account.
  • Are the LeetCode solutions free? Yes, the majority of our LeetCode explanations and solutions are completely free to access.
  • Can I request a specific tutorial? Absolutely! Use the Feature Request section below to suggest new content.

3. Report a Bug

If you encounter any technical issues, broken links, or visual glitches while using our website, please let us know so we can fix them immediately.

  • Please include the URL of the page where you found the bug.
  • Provide a clear description of what happened versus what you expected to happen.
  • If possible, attach screenshots to help our team identify the issue faster.

4. Feature Request

We are constantly looking to improve CodeByTushu and provide content that you find valuable. If you have an idea for a new feature, a specific course topic, or a tutorial you'd like to see, we want to hear about it!

Your feedback shapes the future of our platform. Don't hesitate to share your suggestions with us via email.

5. Business & Collaboration

Are you looking to collaborate, sponsor content, or discuss business opportunities? We are open to partnerships that align with our mission of empowering developers.

  • Sponsorships for YouTube videos or blog posts.
  • Guest posting opportunities.
  • Freelance video editing or web development inquiries.

6. Contact Support

If you need any help or have any questions, feel free to contact us. We'll be happy to assist you.

  • Email: contact@codebytushu.com
  • Response Time: Within 24–48 hours
