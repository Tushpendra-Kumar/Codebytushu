Best Export Settings for Reels

A Professional Guide for High-Quality Short Video Exports

1. Introduction

Short-form content like Reels and Shorts needs optimized export settings for best quality and reach.

---

2. Beginner Level

Ideal Settings

  • Aspect Ratio: 9:16
  • Resolution: 1080 × 1920
  • Format: MP4
  • Frame Rate: 30 FPS
  • Codec: H.264
  • Bitrate: 8–12 Mbps
Correct settings = Better quality + more reach 🚀

Common Mistakes

  • Wrong aspect ratio (16:9)
  • Low bitrate (blurry video)
  • Incorrect resolution
---

3. Intermediate Level

  • Match timeline settings with export
  • Maintain consistent FPS
  • Export slightly higher quality (platform compress karega)

Recommended

  • Bitrate: 10–16 Mbps
  • Audio: AAC, 320 kbps
---

4. Advanced Level

Compression Logic

Platforms like Instagram compress videos — isliye thoda high quality export karo.

Bitrate Strategy

  • Low bitrate = blur ❌
  • High bitrate = large file ⚠
  • Best = 10–20 Mbps ✅

Pro Settings

  • Keyframe: 1–2 sec
  • Color Space: Rec.709
  • Use VBR (Variable Bitrate)
---

5. Practical Examples

  • Low bitrate → blurry reel
  • Correct export → sharp output
  • Wrong ratio → crop issue
